1. Accessing the System
Open the web application.
On the homepage, choose "Register" or "Log-in".

2. Creating an Account (Sign-Up)
Open the Sign-Up page.
Enter the required details:
Personal Information
Water Account Information
Contact Information
Password
Validate your mobile number.
Click "Confirm" to finish registration.

3. Logging In
Enter your email address.
Enter your password.
Click "Login".
You will be redirected to the dashboard.

4. Dashboard Overview
Once logged in, you can:
View Pay Your Bill
Check Water Interruptions
Read News
Navigate all menu sections through the sidebar or burger menu

5. Paying Your Water Bill
Click "Pay your Bill" on the dashboard.
Review your payment details.
Choose your preferred payment method.
Confirm your payment.
Upload your receipt.
Review your updated billing status.

6. Viewing Payment History
Open the Payment History page.
Review all previous payment records.

7. Requesting Services
Go to Services from the navigation menu.
View all available services.
Select a service.
Fill out the provided service form.
Click "Submit Report" to send your request.

8. Viewing Water Interruptions
Open the Water Interruptions section.
Read the latest updates and announcements regarding outages or interruptions.

9. About Section
Go to the About page.
View information about the organization and its services.

10. Managing Your Profile
Click the burger menu and select "Profile".
View your account details.
Click Edit Profile to update your information.
Modify the fields you want to change.
Click "Save" to apply changes.

11. Viewing Consumption History
In the menu, click "Consumption History".
View your monthly water consumption records.

13. Requesting Disconnection
Open the burger menu and click "Disconnection".
Download the disconnection form.
Submit a disconnection request through the provided steps.

13. Logging Out
Open the burger menu and click "Log-Out".
Confirm the action to exit your account.
